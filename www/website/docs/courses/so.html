StarOffice is a powerful office suite offered by Sun Microsystems in order to provide an integrated environment to run the tools organisations need to be more effective and productive. With StarOffice, you have a robust and scalable integrated office productivity suite that includes word processing, spread sheets, email, graphics, web publishing, scheduling, database and management applications. INTENSITY: About 50% of the course is practicals, and 50% lectures.

RESOURCE: Each student will have exclusive use of a Sun workstation for the duration of the course. Each student will be provided with a full set of training notes relating to the course.

Printer(s) are provided on this course so that students can learn how to use them, and also take away printed copies of their work.

PREREQUISITES: Basic keyboard skills are desirable. Some previous use of a word processor would be an advantage. Please call if you require any advice.

FOLLOW UPS: We have a wide range of other courses available.

PRACTICALS / TAKEAWAYS: Each student leaves the course with his own set of training notes for the material covered; around 120 pages per day of training.

Every student who attends this course at our Harwell training centre will be issued with a signed certificate of course completion, which we will be happy to "authenticate" upon future request.

Students on this course can print out their work and take it away with them.

SOFTWARE VERSIONS: Covers StarOffice on Solaris, Microsoft Windows, and Linux operating systems.

 

TOPICS COVERED ON THE COURSE

StarOffice Writer
The StarWriter window. Managing the menu bars and split screen. Use of desktops. Open save and print files. Change of document view. Use of templates. Editing text. Entering special characters. Use of grammar and spell checkers. Format text and paragraphs, use styles. Create bullets and numbered lists. Insert fields. Create and edit tables. Use of drawing functions, including Text Boxes and Text Animation. Insert drawings, spreadsheets and graphs. Usimg mailmerge facilities. Convert MS Word files to StarOffice.

StarOffice Calc
Understanding the StarOffice Calc window. Managing menu bars and split screen. Open save and print files. Navigate and change the Spreadsheet view. Enter and edit data and formulae. Use number and character styles. Protect cells. Print and format the output. Work with multiple spreadsheets. Product charts from Spreadsheets. Modify charts. Convert MS Excel files to StarOffice. email spreadsheets to others.
