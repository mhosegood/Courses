Small Print - Scheduled Courses

Purchase orders must be received before a course can be confirmed. Please make them out to First Alternative.

Invoices will normally be raised upon completion of each course attended, even when you book a series of courses.

Scheduled course fees include lunch and refreshments, but not travel and accommodation.

VAT must be added to all quoted prices at the standard rate, currently 17.5%.

If you wish to cancel or postpone a course, you must do so in writing no less than 10 clear working days before the commencement date, or full payment will be due.

Course descriptions are provided on this site and are available upon request. It is up to you to ensure all attendees meet the prerequisites for attending any course, especially those beyond the basic level. We reserve the right to cancel attendance and refund in full the monies paid for anyone who does not meet the minimum skill requirements.

Substitution of delegates may be made at any time, but please inform our training administrator.
