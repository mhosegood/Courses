Overview of our StarOffice Courses

StarOffice is Sun's Integrated environment - available at no charge on Microsoft, Unix and Linux platforms. With many years of experience training on Sun's products, First Alternative is ideally placed to provide Star quality StarOffice training.

This page includes a short description of the first public courses we are offering on StarOffice, and links to full descriptions of them.

Please select this link for a list of all our scheduled courses.

 

StarOffice

Our StarOffice course covers StarOffice Writer (the equivalent to MS Word) and StarOffice Calc (the equivalent to MS Excel) - perhaps the two most important modules of StarOffice. Approximately one day is spent on each. Plenty of practical hands on provided!

If you have three or more staff to train, may we suggest you visit our custom department for information about extra modules and on-site training?
